# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Overview
This is a personal developer tool used exclusively by its owner to upload videos to their own TikTok account. It is not a public service or commercial product.

## Data Collection
This tool does not collect, store, or process any personal data from any user other than the developer/owner themselves.

## Authentication
OAuth 2.0 tokens used for TikTok authentication are stored locally on the developer's own machine and are never transmitted to or stored on any third-party server.

## Third-Party Sharing
No data of any kind is shared with third parties.
