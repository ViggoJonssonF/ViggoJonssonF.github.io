Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

UEFN Video Poster is a private, personal tool. It does not collect, store, or share data from any third party.

What data is used

The tool uses OAuth tokens for YouTube and TikTok to post videos on behalf of the owner. These tokens are stored privately and never transmitted to anyone other than the respective platforms.

Third parties

No data is sold or shared with third parties. The tool interacts only with the YouTube Data API and TikTok Content Posting API on behalf of the owner.
